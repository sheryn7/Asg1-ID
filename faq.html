How should I reheat my cookies?

Reheat the cookies in an oven or air fryer at 180°C for 2-3 minutes or until heated through. Enjoy them after they have cooled down and turned crispy.

How long can I keep the cookies?

Big Cookies

Keep at room temperature for 1 day. For longer storage, keep in the fridge (airtight container) for up to 3 days, or in the freezer for up to 1 month. Always reheat before eating.

Mini Crispy Cookies

Keep at room temperature for up to 2 weeks. Store in an airtight container after opening.

All of our cookies are baked fresh with no added preservatives.
